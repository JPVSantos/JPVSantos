Special Interests

Cars

Aside from coding, my other passion is cars. I've always been a fan of cars, specifically Japanese imports or JDM. My interest in cars started when I was younger and saw my cousin's 1996 Mitsubishi Lancer in the Bangkok Blue Pearl. Much like the start of my journey with computers, I didn't know much then but I knew that his car was low and loud. I remember him coming over our house and all of us being able to hear him from down the street, a quality I sought after when I got older. Thanks to my exposure at a young age, my favorite car was and always will be a Mitsubish Lancer.
